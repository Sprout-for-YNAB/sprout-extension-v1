Settings

Show/Hide Balances & Totals

Show or hide account and/or category balances/totals from autocomplete.

Theme

Match system will follow whatever theme is set on your system (if supported).

Save last accessed budget

Save your last opened budget. If this is enabled, Sprout for YNAB will automatically open to the budget you last opened. This will save the ID and name of your last opened budget in storage.

Data cache duration

Set how many hours to temporarily save your budget information for faster loading before refreshing from YNAB.

Clear cached data

The last accessed budget and any budget information currently cached will be erased and will be refreshed next time you open Sprout for YNAB. Data cached will include budget IDs, names, and each budget's respective settings, accounts, payees and categories.

Disconnect from Sprout for YNAB

Disconnect your YNAB account and clear all data from Sprout for YNAB.

Your YNAB account has been disconnected from Sprout for YNAB. Thank you for using Sprout for YNAB!

To deauthorize Sprout for YNAB from your account, go to your YNAB Account Settings, scroll down to Authorized Applications and click Revoke on Sprout for YNAB.
